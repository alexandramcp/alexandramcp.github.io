Research

My research explores how equity, education, and systems design can improve health outcomes for children and marginalised populations.

Global Health Equity & Ethics

Description
My work in global health focuses on equity, ethics, and representation—particularly how systems, narratives, and policies shape health outcomes for vulnerable populations. This includes research on decolonising global health, ethical imagery, and the structural determinants of health.

Publications

  • The use of imagery in global health: a framework to guide practice (Lancet Global Health)
  • Decolonising Visual Narratives in Global Health (Springer book chapter)
  • Inequities in managing and representing Mpox (BMJ)
  • UK aid cuts threaten global child health (BMJ Paediatrics Open)
  • Barriers and facilitators to the recruitment of disabled people to clinical trials (BMC Trials)

Presentations & Conferences

  • RCPCH Conference – Oral: Global health imagery and equity
  • Development Engagement Lab – Oral: Ethical imagery in global health
  • Institute of Medical Ethics Conference – Poster: Equity through imagery
  • International Child Health Group Webinar – Global health narratives

Prizes

  • WHO Collaboration Centre Prize for Public Health Education
  • Institute of Medical Ethics Elective Grant
  • Oxford University research grant (imagery ethics work)

Child Health and Vulnerable Groups

Description
I am particularly interested in improving healthcare access and outcomes for unaccompanied asylum-seeking children (UASC). My work integrates infectious disease, mental health, and service design to promote holistic, equitable care models.

Publications

  • Mental health screening in unaccompanied asylum-seeking children in the NHS (Primary Health Care Research & Development)
  • Improving safeguarding of unaccompanied migrant young people (BMJ)
  • Infectious disease screening outcomes & reducing barriers to care for UASC (BMJ Paediatrics Open)

Presentations & Conferences

  • RCPCH Conference – Oral & Poster: UASC screening and care
  • Thames Valley QIP Conference – RHS-13 mental health pathway
  • Reading Pathological Society – Oral: UASC mental health screening
  • North Thames Paediatric Network – The Journey of a UASC

Prizes

  • Adam Snape Prize (best audit/QI project)
  • International Child Health Group Elective Award

Paediatric Infectious Disease

Description
My research explores infectious disease in children and young people, particularly in global and underserved contexts. This includes tuberculosis, vaccination uptake, and the intersection of infection with broader health systems.

Publications

  • Adolescents and young adults with TB in a low-incidence setting (IJTLD Open)
  • Disruptions to routine childhood vaccination during COVID-19 (Frontiers in Pediatrics)
  • Reasons for reductions in routine childhood immunisation uptake (PLOS Global Public Health)

Presentations & Conferences

  • European Society of Paediatric Infectious Diseases (ESPID)
  • European Academy of Paediatric Societies (EAPS)
  • Groote Schuur Hospital Journal Club – TB cohort study
  • St Mary’s Hospital Journal Club – vaccination research

Prizes

  • Best Poster Award – Cambridge Obstetrics & Gynaecology Conference
  • Distinctions in Clinical, Medical, and Scientific assessments

Medical Education & Educational Innovation

Description
I have a strong interest in medical education research, particularly in peer-led teaching, curriculum design, and innovative learning tools. My work focuses on improving student confidence, inclusivity, and the transition to clinical practice.

Publications

  • Animations for teaching cardiac electrophysiology (JMIR Medical Education)
  • Near-peer teaching for foundation programme preparation (Advances in Medical Education & Practice)
  • Burnout in early-year medical students (BMC Medical Education)
  • Community partnerships in medical education (Public Health Challenges)

Presentations & Conferences

  • AMEE Conference – Oral: educational innovation
  • ASME & JASME Conferences – peer teaching and OSCE education
  • Transform Medical Education Conference – community-based education
  • Imperial College Teaching Conference – Keynote speaker

Prizes

  • MedAll Exceptional Educator Award
  • JASME Innovation Prize

Health Systems, Leadership & Behavioural Interventions

Description
My work also explores how healthcare systems and interventions can be improved through leadership, behaviour change, and patient-centred approaches, including coaching and curriculum reform.

Publications

  • Leadership training in healthcare: a systematic umbrella review (BMJ Leader)
  • Health coaching to support patients with addiction (BMJ)
  • Sustainability in primary care education (BMJ Leader)

Presentations & Conferences

  • Reimagining Primary Care Education Think Tank – invited talk
  • Imperial Medical Education Masterclass – invited talk on AI & healthcare
